Before the Pages Began

The First Storytellers

Centuries before European settlers, the Mississippian people lived here (800 CE to 1600s), building complex chiefdoms and earthen mounds for ceremonies[cite: 340, 341, 345].

They were the first to introduce music and sound through drumming and chanting[cite: 348].

A Place Takes Root

The Birth of a Settlement

Settlement began in 1779 with Fort Nashborough, acting as a gateway for families like the Johnsons to establish permanent homes[cite: 376, 380].

The Smith Mansion

Homes like Ravenswood reflect the evolution from log cabins to formal Federal-style residences[cite: 399, 403].

Music & Pop Culture

Brentwood became a "safe haven" for artists like Taylor Swift and Dolly Parton to live away from the spotlight[cite: 302, 306].
